# SurakshaAI – AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Workers

## Overview

SurakshaAI is an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** designed to protect gig workers such as food delivery partners from income loss caused by external disruptions like extreme weather, high pollution levels, or government restrictions.

Unlike traditional insurance, SurakshaAI automatically triggers payouts when predefined conditions are met, eliminating the need for manual claim filing. The goal is to create a **financial safety net for gig workers who depend on daily earnings**.

---

# Problem Statement

Gig workers face significant income instability due to conditions beyond their control, such as:

* 🌧 Heavy rainfall
* 🔥 Extreme heat waves
* 🌫 High pollution levels
* 🚧 Government restrictions or curfews
* 📉 Platform downtime

Most gig workers do **not have financial protection** during these disruptions. SurakshaAI aims to solve this problem by providing **affordable micro-insurance tailored for gig workers**.

---

# Solution

SurakshaAI uses **parametric insurance + AI risk prediction** to automatically compensate workers when disruptions occur.

The system monitors **real-time external conditions** and triggers payouts automatically when predefined thresholds are crossed.

### Key Features

* 🤖 AI-based risk prediction
* 💰 Weekly micro-premium model
* 🌍 Real-time disruption monitoring
* ⚡ Automatic claim triggering
* 📊 Worker dashboard

---

## 🔄 System Workflow

1️⃣ **Worker registers on the platform**  
2️⃣ **AI analyzes risk** based on location and environmental factors  
3️⃣ **Policy with weekly premium** is generated  
4️⃣ **APIs continuously monitor** weather and pollution data  
5️⃣ **Parametric triggers detect** disruptions  
6️⃣ **Automatic payout** is issued when conditions are met

---

# Features

## 1️⃣ User Onboarding

Workers register using:

* Mobile number
* Delivery platform (Swiggy, Zomato, etc.)
* Work location

---

## 2️⃣ AI Risk Profiling

Machine learning models analyze:

* Historical weather data
* Location-based risk factors
* Delivery activity trends

The system assigns a **risk score** to determine coverage and pricing.

---

## 3️⃣ Weekly Micro-Premium Model

The premium model is designed specifically for gig workers' **weekly income cycle**.

Example:

Base Premium: ₹25/week
High Risk Zone Adjustment: +₹10
Low Risk Zone Adjustment: -₹5

Final Premium Range:
**₹20 – ₹35 per week**

---

## 4️⃣ Real-Time Monitoring

External APIs track real-world conditions:

* 🌧 Rainfall
* 🌡 Temperature
* 🌫 Air Quality Index (AQI)
* 🚦 Traffic disruptions

---

## 5️⃣ Automatic Claim Trigger

If disruption conditions exceed predefined thresholds (for example heavy rainfall), the system automatically detects it and **initiates payout without manual claim filing**.

---

# Parametric Triggers

| Trigger Type   | Condition                | Impact                |
| -------------- | ------------------------ | --------------------- |
| Heavy Rain     | Rainfall above threshold | Delivery slowdown     |
| Extreme Heat   | Temperature > 40°C       | Reduced working hours |
| High Pollution | AQI > 300                | Health risk           |
| Curfew         | Government restrictions  | Work halted           |
| App Downtime   | Platform outage          | Order interruption    |
